WELCOME TO IFEMUN 2019

Dear delegates, faculty advisors, and members of the IFEMUN community, I gladly welcome you to the website of the Ife Model United Nations 2019(IFEMUN2019). IFEMUN is an annual conference hosted by the partnership of Obafemi Awolowo University, All Nigeria United Nations Student Association (ANUNSA) OAU chapter, Model United Nations Society, OAU and International Relations Student Association (IRSA).

The first edition which held last year brought hundreds of bright, motivated young people together to discuss and debate some of the world's most pressing issues. IFEMUN 2019 will take place from 30th of October to 2nd of November 2019 in Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife. Over 500 students are expected to congregate in Ile-Ife to play the roles of National representatives to the United Nations and various specialized agencies, debating issues on

an international scale.We have worked to create the most realistic simulation possible, in order to facilitate an engaging and impactful experience for delegates.

We are also continuously striving to expand the diversity of our delegate pool and committee selection, while working to improve the quality of debate and conference practices in order to provide the best experience possible for our delegates.

We would like to invite you to be a part of this exciting journey and hope to see you at IFEMUN 2019.

Sincerely,

Oluwatola Victor Abishola

Why IFEMUN 2019?

Innovation

Through our policy recommendations and resolutions reached at the conference, we tend to provide solutions to global issues

Realism & Quality

We make the simulation as real as the actual working of the UN, the difference is just that it is not done at the UN headquarters. This was why it was dubbed one of the finest MUN conferences in Africa by UN officials. We also provide quality simulation with the wealth of experience of the secretariat members and committee officials.

Diversity

We bring people together from different countries, culture and diverse background to represent assigned countries/other international actors. Delegates are also exposed to several activities during the conference,ranging from diplomatic talks to social events, etc
